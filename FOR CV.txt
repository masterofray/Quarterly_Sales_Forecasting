Project scope includes


As Lead Data Scientist you will:

-       Manage a team of data scientists and data analysts – the scope of works of the team includes collaboration with data scientists, machine learning engenieers or big data specialists of Relyens digital partners -,

-       Build analytics systems and predictive models,

-       Administrate the data products projects management.

You should have a strong problem-solving ability and a knack for statistical analysis to best be able to align data products with our business goals.

Your ultimate goal will be to help improve our products and business decisions by making the most out of our data.


Main Responsibilities

-       Manage a team of data scientists, data analyst and possibly completed in the future with machine learning engineers and/or big data specialists,

-       Lead data mining and collection procedures,

-       Ensure data quality and integrity,

-       Interpret and analyze data problems,

-       Conceive, plan and prioritize data projects,

-       Build analytic systems and predictive models,

-       Responsible for test performance, roll-out and industrialization as well as of reliability of data-driven products,

Responsabilities:


    Own the full Data Science life-cycle from conception to prototyping, testing, deploying, and measuring its overall business value
    Develop quantitative models, leveraging machine learning and advanced data analysis techniques

    Leverage our work in order to increase adoption across our business partners, to drive real business value

Such Goals Include

    Selecting the best financial products for your needs
    Taking the right actions to improve your credit score
    Anticipate your future financial health based on your current financial status and history

With these goals in mind, our Data Scientists use the latest cloud technologies and machine learning tools in order to exploit the potential of data analytics. We always have new and interesting projects on the horizon that aim to help our users reach their personal finance aspirations!

Expected work schedule is 50 hours/week Monday to Friday. In case you will be working from outside the US, please be aware this position requires an overlap with EST business hours.
